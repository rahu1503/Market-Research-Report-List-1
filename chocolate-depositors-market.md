<p><p>Chocolate depositors are machines used in the confectionery industry to deposit chocolate into molds or onto products. There are three main types of chocolate depositors in the market. The pump-driven depositors use pumps to push the chocolate into the molds, providing a consistent flow. Servo-driven depositors use servo motors to control the depositing process, ensuring precise and accurate filling. Other types of depositors include those with special functionalities like decorating with patterns or dual filling capabilities. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different requirements of chocolate manufacturers.</p></p>
