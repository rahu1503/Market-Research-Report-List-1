<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive servo motor market consists of three main types - positional rotation servo motors, continuous rotation servo motors, and linear servo motors. Positional rotation servo motors are used to control the position of the motor shaft precisely. Continuous rotation servo motors provide continuous rotational motion in either clockwise or counterclockwise direction. Linear servo motors, on the other hand, offer linear motion for applications that require precise positioning along a linear path. These servo motor types serve various purposes in the automotive industry, catering to different motion control requirements.</p></p>
