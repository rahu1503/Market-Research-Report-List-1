<p><p>Silicone Mould Release Agents market can be divided into three types: Polyalkylene Glycol (PAG), Polyethylene Glycol (PEG), and others. PAG-based mould release agents are widely used due to their excellent ability to withstand high temperatures and their compatibility with various molding materials. PEG-based agents are popular for their low toxicity and environmental friendliness. The "others" category includes alternative release agents such as silicones, waxes, and oils. Each type has its own unique characteristics, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable agent based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
