<p><p>The camera module assembly equipment market is categorized into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic equipment refers to systems that operate independently with minimal human intervention. These machines perform tasks such as component placement, soldering, and testing without human assistance, providing higher production efficiency and accuracy. On the other hand, semi-automatic equipment requires some human involvement, usually for loading and unloading components or conducting quality checks. While not as efficient as automatic systems, semi-automatic equipment offers more flexibility and affordability for smaller production volumes.</p></p>
