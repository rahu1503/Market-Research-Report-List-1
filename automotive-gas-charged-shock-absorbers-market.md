<p><ul><li>Mono Tube</li><li>Twin Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive gas charged shock absorbers are used to control the suspension system in vehicles and provide a smooth and comfortable ride. Mono-tube shock absorbers have a single tube with both the gas and piston in it, allowing for better heat dissipation and performance. Twin-tube shock absorbers have two tubes, with the inner tube containing the piston and valve system, while the outer tube holds the damping fluid and gas. Twin-tube shocks offer better durability and are commonly used in most vehicles. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different vehicle applications.</p></p>
