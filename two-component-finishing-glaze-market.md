<p><p>The two-component finishing glaze market comprises two types: oil-based finishing glaze and water-based finishing glaze. Water-based finishing glaze, on the other hand, is made from water, resins, and additives. This variant has a lower VOC content, dries faster, and is environmentally friendly.
