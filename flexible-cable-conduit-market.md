<p><p>Flexible cable conduit is available in two main types: metal material and non-metallic material. The metal material market refers to conduits made of metals like steel, aluminum, or copper. These conduits are known for their durability, high temperature resistance, and excellent electromagnetic shielding properties. On the other hand, the non-metallic material market includes conduits made of materials such as PVC, nylon, or polyethylene. These conduits are lighter, more flexible, and resistant to corrosion and chemicals. Both types of conduits offer different benefits, catering to the specific requirements of various industries and applications.</p></p>
