<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Tire Pressure Management Systems (TPMS) are categorized into three types: detecting system, alarm system, and other market. </p><p>The detecting system monitors the air pressure in tires and provides real-time feedback to the driver. It ensures proper inflation, reduces fuel consumption, and prevents accidents caused by underinflated tires.</p><p>The alarm system alerts the driver when tire pressure falls below the recommended level, preventing potential accidents and increasing vehicle efficiency.</p><p>The "other market" category includes additional features like temperature monitoring, data logging, and wireless connectivity, further enhancing tire safety and performance.</p></p>
