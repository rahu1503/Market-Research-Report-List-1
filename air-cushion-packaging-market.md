Air pillow cushions are inflatable plastic pouches filled with air, which provide excellent protection for fragile items during shipping and handling. On the other hand, bubble wrap machines produce sheets or rolls of plastic material filled with air-filled bubbles. These bubbles serve as a protective layer around products, preventing damage from impact or vibration. Both air pillow cushions and bubble wrap machines are popular choices in the packaging industry for their effectiveness in safeguarding goods.</p></p>
