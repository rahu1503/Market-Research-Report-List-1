<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive EVP (Electric Vacuum Pump) market consists of three main types: diaphragm type, leaf type, and swing piston type. The diaphragm type EVP uses a flexible diaphragm to generate vacuum by expanding and contracting. On the other hand, the leaf type EVP utilizes multiple leaf-like segments that create vacuum by flexing. Lastly, the swing piston type EVP employs a reciprocating piston that generates vacuum by swinging back and forth. These types of EVPs play a significant role in ensuring proper functioning of various automotive systems, such as brake boosters and emission controls.</p></p>
