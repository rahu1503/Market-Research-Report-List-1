<p><p>The waste or garbage collection vehicle market consists of various types of vehicles used for collecting and disposing of waste. Front loaders are equipped with a mechanical arm at the front to lift and empty dumpsters. Rear loaders have a hydraulic compactor at the rear, where waste is loaded manually before being compacted. Side loaders allow waste to be loaded from the side, using a manual or automated system. Grapple trucks use a claw-like apparatus to pick up and transport bulky waste. Each type of vehicle has its own advantages and is employed based on specific collection needs.</p></p>
