<p><p>Mulberry Silks Market Types refer to two different methods of obtaining silk: Wild Growth Mulberry Silk and Artificial Rearing Mulberry Silk. Wild Growth Mulberry Silk comes from worms that feed on naturally grown mulberry leaves in the wild. This type of silk is considered to be of higher quality due to the worms' natural diet. On the other hand, Artificial Rearing Mulberry Silk is produced from worms that are reared in controlled environments and fed with cultivated mulberry leaves. This type of silk is more standardized and widely available.</p></p>
