<p><p>Masking tape is a versatile adhesive tape used mainly in painting and other applications where temporary adhesion is required. There are several types of masking tape available in the market. Polyester-based masking tape offers high strength and resistance to solvents, making it suitable for automotive and electronic applications. Polyimide-based masking tape has excellent heat resistance, making it ideal for electrical and electronic insulation. Glass-cloth masking tape is reinforced with fiberglass and is commonly used in high-temperature and high-strength applications. Vinyl masking tape is flexible and easy to remove, making it suitable for general-purpose applications. Other types of masking tape include paper-based and plastic-based tapes.</p></p>
