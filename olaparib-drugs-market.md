<p><p>Olaparib, also known by its brand name Lynparza, is a targeted therapy drug classified as a poly ADP ribose polymerase (PARP) inhibitor. It is primarily used in the treatment of advanced ovarian cancer and certain types of breast cancer. Olaparib works by inhibiting the activity of the PARP enzymes, which are involved in DNA repair in cancer cells. By blocking these enzymes, Olaparib helps to prevent cancer cells from repairing their damaged DNA, leading to their death.</p><p>The future outlook for Olaparib drugs market is promising.
