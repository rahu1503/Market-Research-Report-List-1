<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The GaAs Polished Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LEC Grown GaAs</li><li>VGF Grown GaAs</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaAs polished wafer market consists of various types, including LEC (Liquid Encapsulated Czochralski) grown GaAs, VGF (Vertical Gradient Freeze) grown GaAs, and others. LEC grown GaAs is produced through the Czochralski process, where the crystal growth is achieved by pulling a seed crystal out of a melt. VGF grown GaAs, on the other hand, is produced using a vertical gradient freeze method that results in improved crystal quality. The "others" category comprises other methods used to grow GaAs wafers, such as molecular beam epitaxy and metalorganic vapor phase epitaxy.</p></p>
