<p><p>Haemofilters are medical devices used for blood purification in patients suffering from kidney failure or other renal disorders. The Haemofilters market is categorized into two types; disposable and reusable. Disposable haemofilters are designed for single-use and are discarded after each procedure, while reusable haemofilters can undergo sterilization and be used for multiple treatment sessions. Both types offer benefits and drawbacks, with disposable haemofilters providing convenience and reduced infection risk, while reusable haemofilters offer cost-effectiveness and reduced waste generation. The choice between the two depends on factors such as patient needs, healthcare facility preferences, and economic considerations.</p></p>
