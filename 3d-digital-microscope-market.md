<p><p>The 3D Digital Microscope market consists of two main types: Motorized Lens and Manual Lens. Motorized Lens microscopes are equipped with motors that allow users to easily adjust the focus and zoom levels digitally, enhancing precision and convenience. On the other hand, Manual Lens microscopes require manual adjustments for focusing and zooming, providing a more hands-on experience. While Motorized Lens microscopes are favored for their ease of use and accuracy, Manual Lens microscopes appeal to users who prefer a traditional approach and more control over their observations.</p></p>
