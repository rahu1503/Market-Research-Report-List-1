<p><p>Pad-mounted switchgear refers to electrical equipment that is used to control and distribute power in outdoor substations. There are two types of pad-mounted switchgear available in the market: gas-insulated and air-insulated switchgear. Gas-insulated switchgear uses sulfur hexafluoride gas to insulate and cool the electrical components, providing excellent electrical insulation properties. Air-insulated switchgear, on the other hand, relies on air as the insulation medium. Both types of switchgear are used to ensure reliable power distribution and protection against electrical faults in outdoor environments.</p></p>
