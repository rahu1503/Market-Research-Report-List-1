<p><p>PVDC latex is a type of latex polymer that is commonly used in various industries for its exceptional barrier properties. There are two main types of PVDC latex: emulsion PVDC latex and solution PVDC latex. Emulsion PVDC latex is a milky white liquid, made by dispersing PVDC polymer particles in water, and it is primarily used for coating applications. On the other hand, solution PVDC latex is a clear liquid, created through the dissolving of PVDC in a solvent, and it is mainly utilized for adhesive applications. Both types offer excellent barrier resistance against gases, vapors, and liquids, making them ideal for packaging, textile, and automotive industries.</p></p>
