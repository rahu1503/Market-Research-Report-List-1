<p><p>The High Voltage Motor Terminal Box market can be divided into two types: Traditional and PSTB. Traditional terminal boxes are the conventional types that have been used for many years and typically require manual wiring and installation. On the other hand, PSTB (Process Switch Terminal Box) is a newer type of terminal box that offers advanced features like pre-wiring and plug-and-play installation, making it faster, easier, and more efficient to set up high voltage motor connections. These types cater to different preferences and requirements in the motor terminal box market.</p></p>
