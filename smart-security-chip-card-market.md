<p><p>The Smart Security Chip Card market consists of two types: Contact and Contactless. Contact cards require physical insertion into a card reader for transactions or accessing information. These cards have a built-in microchip that securely stores data and requires direct contact with the reader. On the other hand, Contactless cards utilize radio frequency technology to transmit data wirelessly to a reader. They can be simply tapped or waved near the reader for transactions, providing a more convenient and faster experience. Both types ensure secure data storage and protect against fraudulent activities.</p></p>
