<p><p>Assembly adhesives are used to join or bond various materials together. There are two types of assembly adhesives: paste and tape. Paste adhesives are a thick, viscous substance that is applied to the surfaces being joined. They provide strong, durable bonds and are commonly used in industrial applications. On the other hand, tape adhesives are pre-cut into strips or rolls and are easy to handle and apply. They are suitable for temporary or lightweight bonding applications. Both paste and tape adhesives offer advantages depending on the specific needs of the project.</p></p>
