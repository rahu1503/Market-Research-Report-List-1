<p><p>The UV-LED curable ink market consists of three major types: offset printing, flexo printing, and digital printing. Offset printing is a traditional method that transfers ink from a plate to a rubber blanket and then onto the printing surface. Flexo printing uses flexible relief plates to transfer ink onto a variety of materials. Digital printing involves directly transferring ink onto the material without the need for plates. These three types cater to different printing needs, providing options for high-quality, versatile, and cost-effective printing solutions.</p></p>
