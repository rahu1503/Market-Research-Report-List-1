<p><p>The pre-dispersed additives market consists of various types, including EPDM-based, SBR-based, NBR-based, natural rubber-based, and others. EPDM-based pre-dispersed additives are composed of ethylene propylene diene monomer rubber and are commonly used for enhancing properties of rubber compounds. SBR-based pre-dispersed additives are made from styrene-butadiene rubber and are utilized for improving the durability and strength of rubber products. NBR-based pre-dispersed additives are manufactured from nitrile butadiene rubber and are known for their excellent oil and fuel resistance. Natural rubber-based pre-dispersed additives are derived from natural rubber and are preferred for their high flexibility and tensile strength. The "others" category includes various types of pre-dispersed additives that provide specific performance-enhancing properties to rubber compounds.</p></p>
