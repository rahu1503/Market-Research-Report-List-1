<p><p>The Surveillance Lens market includes two main types: C-Mount and CS-Mount lenses. C-Mount lenses are the standard type commonly used in surveillance cameras. They have a 1-inch diameter thread and can be easily attached to different camera types. On the other hand, CS-Mount lenses are smaller and have a 1/2-inch diameter thread. They require a CS-Mount adapter to be used with C-Mount cameras. Both C-Mount and CS-Mount lenses play a crucial role in surveillance systems, enabling the capture of high-quality images and videos for security purposes.</p></p>
