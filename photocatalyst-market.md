<p><ul><li>TiO2 Base</li><li>ZnO Base</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photocatalyst market can be divided into three primary types: TiO2 base, ZnO base, and Others. TiO2 base photocatalysts are primarily based on titanium dioxide and are widely utilized due to their excellent catalytic properties. ZnO base photocatalysts, on the other hand, are based on zinc oxide and have gained popularity for their notable photocatalytic efficiency. The "Others" category encompasses a range of photocatalysts utilizing materials other than TiO2 or ZnO, such as metal oxides or complex compounds. These various types cater to different applications within the field of photocatalysis.</p></p>
