<p><ul><li>Real Time PCR Machine</li><li>Standard PCR Machine</li><li>Digital PCR Machine</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCR machine market comprises different types of machines used in the polymerase chain reaction (PCR) technique. Real-time PCR machines allow for the continuous monitoring and quantification of DNA amplification in real-time, providing faster and more accurate results. Standard PCR machines are traditional models that use end-point detection, suitable for routine PCR applications. Digital PCR machines partition samples into thousands of individual reactions, enabling absolute quantification of DNA targets. Other types include multiplex PCR machines, touchdown PCR machines, and nested PCR machines, each designed for specific research needs.</p></p>
