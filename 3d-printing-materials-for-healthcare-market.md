<p><p>The 3D printing materials for healthcare market consists of various types, such as Polyamide, Peek, Titanium, and Cobalt-Chrome Alloy. Polyamide is a synthetic polymer with high flexibility and durability, suitable for creating prosthetics and orthotics. Peek (Polyether ether ketone) is a biocompatible and heat-resistant material used for implants and dental prosthetics. Titanium is widely used due to its biocompatibility, strength, and lightness for creating medical implants. Cobalt-Chrome Alloy is known for its excellent strength, corrosion resistance, and biocompatibility, making it suitable for dental and orthopedic applications.</p></p>
