<p><p>Sodium silicate glass, also known as water glass or liquid glass, is a compound that consists of sodium oxide (Na2O) and silica (SiO2). Sodium silicate glass has various applications in industries such as automotive, construction, water treatment, textiles, and chemicals.</p><p>In the automotive sector, sodium silicate glass is used as a sealant in the production of tires, helping to increase their durability and resistance to heat. In the construction industry, it is used as an adhesive in the production of cement, providing strength and stability to structures. It is also used in water treatment processes, as it has the ability to precipitate heavy metals and neutralize acidic substances. Additionally, it finds applications in the production of detergents, flame retardants, and catalysts.</p><p>The sodium silicate glass market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
