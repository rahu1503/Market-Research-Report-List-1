<p><p>The epoxy glass market is characterized by various manufacturing techniques. Lay-up involves manually laying layers of epoxy resin and glass fabric onto a mold. Compression molding uses heat and pressure to compress the resin and glass fabric in a mold. Resin injection injects resin into a closed mold. Resin transfer molding utilizes two molds and pressure to transfer resin into a closed mold. Filament winding involves rotating a mandrel while applying resin and glass fibers. Pultrusion pulls continuous fibers through a resin bath, then through a heated die to form a continuous profile.</p></p>
