The increasing adoption of renewable energy sources such as solar and wind power is driving the demand for AC-DC converters, as they are essential for converting the generated AC power to DC power for storage or use by electronic devices. With the increasing adoption of electric vehicles, smart appliances, and energy-efficient lighting solutions, the need for AC-DC converters that can efficiently convert AC power to low-voltage DC power is growing substantially.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology such as the development of GaN (gallium nitride) and SiC (silicon carbide) power devices are further boosting the market growth of AC-DC switching converters. As portable electronic devices such as smartphones, tablets, and wearables continue to gain popularity, the demand for compact and lightweight AC-DC converters that can power these devices efficiently is expected to increase.</p><p>In conclusion, the AC-DC switching converter market is projected to grow at a significant rate of 8.6% during the forecasted period.
