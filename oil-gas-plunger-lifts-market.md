<p><p>The Oil & Gas Plunger Lifts market consists of two main types - Single Pad Plunger Type and Dual Pad Plunger Type. In the Single Pad Plunger Type market, a single plunger is used to lift fluids from one well. This type of plunger lift system is generally cost-effective and suitable for wells with low production rates. On the other hand, the Dual Pad Plunger Type market uses two plungers to lift fluids from two separate wells simultaneously. This type of system is more efficient and ideal for wells with higher production rates.</p></p>
