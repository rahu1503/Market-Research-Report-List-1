<p><p>The solid & dry lubricants market encompasses various types such as MoS2 (Molybdenum Disulfide), PTFE, graphite, soft metals, and others. MoS2 is widely used as it provides excellent lubrication even in extreme temperature and pressure conditions. PTFE, known for its nonstick properties, is used in automotive and industrial applications. Graphite, with its low friction coefficient, is preferred for high-temperature applications. Soft metals like lead and silver offer good lubrication and are used in softer components. Other lubricants like boron nitride and ceramic materials are also used depending on the lubrication requirements.</p></p>
