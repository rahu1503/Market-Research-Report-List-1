<p><p>High speed tablet rotary presses refer to machines used in pharmaceutical production to compress powder or granulated materials into solid tablets at a high speed. There are two main types of high speed tablet rotary presses: single punching machines and multi-punching machines. </p><p>Single punching machines operate by using a single upper punch to compress powder or granules into tablets, resulting in one tablet being produced per rotation. </p><p>On the other hand, multi-punching machines have multiple upper punches, allowing them to produce multiple tablets per rotation. This increases the production capacity and efficiency of the machine.</p></p>
