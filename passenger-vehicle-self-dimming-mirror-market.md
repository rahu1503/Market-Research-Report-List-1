<p><p>The passenger vehicle self-dimming mirror market consists of two types of mirrors: the outer self-dimming mirror and the inside self-dimming mirror. The outer self-dimming mirror is installed on the exterior of the vehicle and automatically adjusts its brightness to reduce glare from headlights of other vehicles behind. This enhances the driver's visibility and reduces eye strain. On the other hand, the inside self-dimming mirror is positioned inside the cabin and automatically dims its reflection when it detects bright lights from the rear to improve the driver's comfort and safety.</p></p>
