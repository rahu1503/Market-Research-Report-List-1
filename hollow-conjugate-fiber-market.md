<p><p>The hollow conjugate fiber market consists of two main types: hollow conjugated siliconized fiber and hollow conjugated non-silicified fiber. Hollow conjugated siliconized fiber is treated with a silicon-based material that gives it a smooth and silky texture, making it ideal for use in filling pillows, cushions, and comforters. On the other hand, hollow conjugated non-silicified fiber is untreated and retains its hollow structure, making it suitable for applications such as insulation, soundproofing, and filtration. Both types offer unique properties and find applications in various industries.</p></p>
