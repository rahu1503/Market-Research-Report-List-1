<p><p>The Medical Video Recorder market comprises three major types: Full HD, High-definition, and UHD (Ultra High Definition). Full HD recorders provide a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels, offering high-quality video capture. High-definition recorders present a slightly lower resolution but still produce clear and detailed visuals. UHD recorders, also known as 4K recorders, deliver exceptional image clarity with a resolution of 3840x2160 pixels. These various types cater to different healthcare settings and procedures, allowing medical professionals to choose the most suitable recording option based on their specific requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
