<p><p>The organ-style paint filter paper market consists of three main types: ordinary filter paper, glass fiber filter paper, and polypropylene filter paper. Ordinary filter paper is the most common type, made from cellulose fibers and used for general paint filtration purposes. Glass fiber filter paper is made from fine glass fibers, offering higher filtration efficiency and resistance to high temperatures. Polypropylene filter paper is made from synthetic fibers, providing excellent chemical resistance and superior filtration performance. Each type caters to different paint filtration needs and varies in terms of cost and functionality.</p></p>
