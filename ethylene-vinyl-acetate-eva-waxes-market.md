<p><p>The Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) waxes market can be divided into two types: non-oxidized EVA waxes and oxidized EVA waxes. Non-oxidized EVA waxes are produced by a process that does not involve oxidation and are used in various applications such as adhesives, coatings, and rubber industries. On the other hand, oxidized EVA waxes are produced by oxidizing non-oxidized EVA waxes to improve their compatibility with other materials, enhance their melting point, and increase their hardness.
