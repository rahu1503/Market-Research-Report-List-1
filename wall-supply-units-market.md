<p><p>Wall supply units are devices used to provide electrical power and connectivity to various electronic devices. There are two main types in the market: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal wall supply units are designed to be installed on walls in a horizontal position, with outlets and USB ports arranged side by side. Vertical wall supply units, on the other hand, are installed in a vertical position, with outlets and USB ports arranged on top of each other. These different types cater to varying installation preferences and aesthetics, providing flexibility in accommodating multiple devices in residential and commercial environments.</p></p>
