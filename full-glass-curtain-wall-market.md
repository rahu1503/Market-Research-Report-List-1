<p><p>The Full Glass Curtain Wall market encompasses three main types: Single Glazed Type, Double Glazed Type, and Three Glazed Type. The Single Glazed Type consists of a single layer of glass, offering basic protection and insulation. The Double Glazed Type incorporates two glass layers with a gap in between filled with gas, providing improved insulation and noise reduction. The Three Glazed Type, as the name suggests, comprises three layers of glass with two gas-filled gaps, delivering superior insulation, noise reduction, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
