The proliferation of smart devices and data-intensive applications has led to a surge in cellular data traffic, putting pressure on existing networks. Small cells and femtocells are seen as an effective solution to address this issue by offloading traffic from macro networks and providing localized coverage.</p><p>Furthermore, the deployment of small cells and femtocells is becoming more cost-effective compared to traditional infrastructure expansion, making it an attractive option for mobile network operators.
