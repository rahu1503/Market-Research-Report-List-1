<p><p>The Non-metallic Coating/Film Thickness Gauge market includes two types: Wet Film Side Thickness and Dry Film Side Thickness gauges. Wet Film Side Thickness gauges are used to measure the thickness of a wet coating before it dries. This helps ensure the proper amount of coating is applied, preventing defects. Dry Film Side Thickness gauges, on the other hand, are used to measure the thickness of a dried coating. This is important for quality control and to ensure the coating meets specifications. Both gauges play a crucial role in maintaining coating application accuracy.</p></p>
