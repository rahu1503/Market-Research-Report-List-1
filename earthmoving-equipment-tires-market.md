<p><p>Earthmoving equipment tires can be categorized into two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have layers of steel cords running perpendicular to the tread direction, providing stability, improved traction, and longer tread life. They are ideal for heavy-duty construction equipment due to their ability to carry heavy loads. On the other hand, bias tires have layers of cords running at specific angles to the tread direction, allowing them to flex and absorb shocks. They are durable and suitable for off-road applications. Both types have their advantages and are used based on specific requirements and conditions.</p></p>
