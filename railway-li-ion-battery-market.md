<p><p>The Railway Li-ion Battery market comprises two major types: LFP Battery and Li-NMC Battery. LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries are known for their superior safety, longevity, and thermal stability. They are widely used in railway applications due to their ability to withstand high temperatures and rough environmental conditions. Li-NMC (Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt Oxide) batteries, on the other hand, offer a higher energy density, enabling longer travel distances without the need for frequent recharging. This makes them suitable for high-performance railway electric vehicles that require extended operating ranges.</p></p>
