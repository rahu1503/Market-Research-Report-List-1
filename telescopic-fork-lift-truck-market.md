<p><p>A Telescopic Fork Lift Truck, also known as a telehandler, is a versatile and powerful machine commonly used in construction, agriculture, and industrial applications. <p><p>The telescopic forklift truck market consists of three main types: compact telescopic forklifts, high reach telescopic forklifts, and heavy lift telescopic forklifts. Compact telescopic forklifts are designed for maneuverability in tight spaces and are commonly used in construction sites and warehouses. High reach telescopic forklifts are designed to access higher elevations, making them ideal for tasks such as stocking shelves in large warehouses. Heavy lift telescopic forklifts, on the other hand, are built to handle heavier loads and are commonly used in industries like mining and oil and gas.</p></p>
