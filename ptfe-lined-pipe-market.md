<p><p>The PTFE lined pipe market offers different types of pipes to cater to various needs. Extruded tube pipes are created by pushing molten PTFE through a die, resulting in a straight, cylindrical shape. Multi-lumen tube pipes have multiple small PTFE-lined channels within a single pipe, offering the ability to transport different fluids simultaneously. Shaped tube pipes are designed with specific bend angles or configurations to fit specific applications. The "Other" market includes PTFE lined pipes with unique features or variations that do not fit into the above-mentioned categories, meeting specialized requirements in the industry.</p></p>
