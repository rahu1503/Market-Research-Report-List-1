<p><p>The wafer back grinding tapes market consists of two main types, namely UV Type and Non-UV Type. UV Type tapes utilize ultraviolet light to cure the adhesive, providing strong bonding properties. These tapes are commonly used in applications where high levels of adhesion are required. On the other hand, Non-UV Type tapes use a different curing mechanism that does not require ultraviolet light. These tapes are preferred in applications where UV exposure is not feasible or necessary and offer similar levels of adhesion and performance.</p></p>
