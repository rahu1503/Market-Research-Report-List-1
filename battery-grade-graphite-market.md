<p><p>The battery-grade graphite market consists of two main types: natural graphite and artificial graphite. Natural graphite is mined from deposits and requires purification processes to enhance its quality for battery applications. It offers good conductivity and higher capacity, making it suitable for lithium-ion batteries in electric vehicles. On the other hand, artificial graphite is produced through the heat treatment of petroleum coke or coal tar pitch. It exhibits excellent stability and lower impurities, making it ideal for high-performance batteries used in aerospace and defense applications.</p></p>
