<p><ul><li>Variable Air Volume (VAV)</li><li>Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV)</li><li>Fan Control Unit (FCU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Air Conditioning Systems market encompasses different types of systems. Variable Air Volume (VAV) systems regulate airflow by adjusting the volume of conditioned air to meet varying cooling demands. Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV) systems utilize refrigerant to cool or heat multiple zones individually, providing greater energy efficiency and flexibility. Fan Control Unit (FCU) systems regulate fan speed and airflow, ensuring optimal cooling and ventilation. These systems offer distinct advantages and are widely used in commercial settings to provide comfortable and energy-efficient air conditioning solutions.</p></p>
