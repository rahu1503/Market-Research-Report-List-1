<p><p>The inflatable rafting boat market consists of three main types: Neoprene, PVC, and Polyurethane. Neoprene rafts are known for their durability and resistance to wear and tear, making them ideal for rough water conditions. PVC rafts are the most common and widely used due to their affordability and versatility. They are generally lightweight and easy to transport. On the other hand, Polyurethane rafts offer exceptional puncture resistance and are commonly used in whitewater rafting. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences, allowing customers to choose the most suitable option for their rafting adventures.</p></p>
