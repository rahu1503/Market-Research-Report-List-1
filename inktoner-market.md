<p><p>The ink and toner market can be categorized into two main types: liquid and powder. Liquid ink is commonly used in inkjet printers and consists of a water-based solution containing color pigments. It is sprayed onto the paper as tiny droplets to create images or text. On the other hand, toner is a powdered substance used in laser printers and photocopiers. It consists of a combination of plastic particles and coloring agents that are electrically charged to create images on paper through a heat transfer process.</p></p>
