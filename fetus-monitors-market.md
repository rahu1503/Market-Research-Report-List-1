<p><p>Fetus monitors are classified into two types, Type I and Type II, based on their functionalities and features. Type I monitors are designed for basic fetal monitoring and provide essential information about the baby's heart rate, uterine contractions, and fetal movements. They are typically used in standard healthcare settings. On the other hand, Type II monitors offer advanced capabilities such as Doppler ultrasound, fetal electrocardiography, and maternal vital signs monitoring. These monitors are commonly found in specialized healthcare facilities with a higher level of care and monitoring requirements.</p></p>
