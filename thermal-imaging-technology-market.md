<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Thermal Imaging Technology Market Types are divided into two categories: uncooled type and cooled type. </p><p>Uncooled type thermal imaging technology does not require cooling mechanisms and uses microbolometer sensors to detect infrared radiation. It is cost-effective, compact, and has lower power consumption. </p><p>Cooled type thermal imaging technology, on the other hand, utilizes a cryogenic cooling system to achieve higher image resolution and sensitivity. It provides detailed and accurate thermal images but is more expensive, larger in size, and requires more power.</p></p>
