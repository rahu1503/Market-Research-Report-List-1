The increasing adoption of low-VOC coatings by various industries, such as automotive, construction, and packaging, is also driving market growth.</p><p>The future outlook of the low-VOC coating additives market is promising. Dispersants enhance the distribution and stability of pigments and fillers in the coating. Wetting agents improve the ability of the coating to spread and adhere to the substrate.
