<p><p>Automotive primary wire is categorized into two main types, namely cross-link automotive wire and PVC automotive wire. Cross-link automotive wire is made from materials that are resistant to high temperatures, moisture, and abrasion. It is commonly used in automotive applications that require durability and reliability. On the other hand, PVC automotive wire is made from polyvinyl chloride, providing good resistance against chemicals and oil. It is suitable for automotive uses that do not require high-temperature resistance. Both types play a crucial role in providing efficient electrical connections in vehicles.</p></p>
