<p><p>Foliar fertilizers are categorized into four types: nitrogenous fertilizer, phosphatic fertilizer, potassic fertilizer, and micronutrients fertilizer. Nitrogenous fertilizers are rich in nitrogen, which is essential for plant growth. Phosphatic fertilizers provide plants with phosphorus, promoting root development and flower production. Potassic fertilizers contain potassium, aiding in disease resistance and fruit quality improvement. Micronutrient fertilizers supply plants with essential trace elements like iron, zinc, and copper, which are necessary for proper growth and development. These fertilizers are applied directly to the leaves and are quickly absorbed by plants for rapid nutrient uptake.</p></p>
