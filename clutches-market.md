<p><p>The clutch market consists of three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and full-automatic. In the manual market, clutches require the driver to physically engage and disengage the clutch by using a third pedal. Semi-automatic clutches combine manual and automatic elements, allowing the driver to shift gears manually or switch to automatic mode. Full-automatic clutches, on the other hand, require no input from the driver as they automatically engage and disengage the clutch based on engine speed and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
