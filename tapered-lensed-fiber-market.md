<p><p>The tapered lensed fiber market refers to the market for fibers that have a tapered shape at one end, where a lens is formed. These fibers can be divided into two main types: single mode fiber and multimode fiber. Single mode fiber is designed to transmit only one mode of light, providing a narrower bandwidth but longer distance transmission. Multimode fiber, on the other hand, allows multiple modes of light to be transmitted simultaneously, offering a wider bandwidth but shorter distance transmission. Both types cater to different applications and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
