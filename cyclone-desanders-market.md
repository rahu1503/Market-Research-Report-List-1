<p><p>Cyclone desanders are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, mining, and construction to remove solid particles from fluids. There are two main market types: Monoblock and Multi-body Parallel. Monoblock desanders consist of a single unit with one inlet and two outlets to separate solids from the fluid. On the other hand, Multi-body Parallel desanders have multiple units operating simultaneously, each containing one inlet and two outlets. Both types are effective in enhancing fluid quality, but the choice depends on the specific needs and scale of the industry.</p></p>
