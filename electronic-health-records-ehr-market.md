<p><p>Electronic Health Records (EHR) market consists of three main types: Individual Health Records, Family Health Records, and Community Health Records. Individual Health Records are created and maintained for each individual patient, containing their medical history, treatments, and health information. Family Health Records are designed to track and manage the health records of an entire family, ensuring easy access and communication between healthcare providers. Community Health Records are interconnected systems that allow healthcare providers to share and access health information within a community, facilitating collaborative care and improving overall healthcare outcomes.</p></p>
