<p><strong>The Pneumatic Piston Vibrator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impacting Piston Vibrators</li><li>Non-Impacting Piston Vibrators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pneumatic Piston Vibrator Market consists of two main types: Impacting Piston Vibrators and Non-Impacting Piston Vibrators. Impacting Piston Vibrators generate vibrations through internal striking mechanisms, resulting in high-intensity impacts. They are commonly used in applications requiring intense shaking or compacting. On the other hand, Non-Impacting Piston Vibrators produce vibrations by reciprocating back and forth without any internal striking parts. They are suitable for situations where a gentler vibrating action is needed, such as conveying bulk materials or separating particles.</p></p>
