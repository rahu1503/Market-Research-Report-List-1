<p><p>The Antifreeze Of Automobiles market consists of several types, including CaCl2, CH3OH, C2H5OH, C2H4(OH)2, and C3H5(OH)3. These substances are commonly used in automobiles to prevent the engine coolant from freezing during low temperatures. CaCl2 is calcium chloride, CH3OH is methanol, C2H5OH is ethanol, C2H4(OH)2 is ethylene glycol, and C3H5(OH)3 is glycerol. Each type has different properties and advantages. CaCl2 provides better protection against freezing, while C2H5OH and CH3OH may be used as alternatives due to their lower toxicity. C2H4(OH)2 and C3H5(OH)3 also offer excellent freeze protection and can be found in some specialized products.</p></p>
