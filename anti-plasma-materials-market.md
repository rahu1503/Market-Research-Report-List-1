<p><p>Anti-plasma materials are specialized coatings or films that are used to protect surfaces from plasma damage. With the increasing prevalence of plasma-based processes, such as plasma etching, plasma deposition, and plasma cutting, the demand for anti-plasma materials is expected to grow significantly.
