By measuring the absorption of the laser beam, the concentration of the gas can be determined.</p><p>The TDLAS-based gas analyzer market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors. <p><p>TDLAS Based Gas Analyser Market Types include In-Situ TDLAS Based Gas Analyser and Extractive TDLAS Based Gas Analyser. In-Situ TDLAS analyzers are mounted directly in the gas stream, continuously measuring the gas properties without the need for sample extraction. On the other hand, Extractive TDLAS analyzers extract a sample of the gas, transport it to a measurement cell and then analyze it. Both types offer accurate and real-time gas analysis capabilities, but differ in their installation and sampling methods.</p></p>
