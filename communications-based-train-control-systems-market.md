<p><p>Communication-based train control systems market types include semi-automatic train operation, driverless train operation, and unattended train operation. </p><p>In semi-automatic train operation, the train is operated by a human driver who receives instructions and commands from the control center via a communication system. </p><p>In driverless train operation, the train operates without any human intervention as it is equipped with advanced technologies such as sensors, cameras, and communication systems that enable it to automatically control its movement. </p><p>In unattended train operation, the train operates without any onboard staff, and all functions are controlled remotely from the control center using communication systems and advanced automation technologies.</p></p>
