<p><p>The animal-based compound feed market offers various types of feed forms to cater to the diverse needs of animals. Mash refers to finely ground feed, which is easily digestible for animals with smaller stomachs. Pellet feed is compacted into small cylindrical shapes through heat and pressure, ensuring stability and reduced waste. Crumble feed is finely broken down into small pieces, suitable for young animals. Other forms include textured feed, extruded feed, and liquid feed, providing different feeding options for animals based on their size, age, and nutritional requirements.</p></p>
