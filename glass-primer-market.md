<p><p>Glass primer is a type of adhesive used to increase the adhesion of coatings, paints, or adhesives on glass surfaces. There are two common types of glass primers: Silane Glass Primer and Urethane Glass Primer. Silane Glass Primer utilizes silane molecules to form a chemical bond between the glass surface and the coating, providing excellent adhesion. On the other hand, Urethane Glass Primer contains urethane compounds that promote adhesion and enhance the durability of the coating or adhesive applied on glass surfaces. Both types offer reliable adhesion properties to ensure optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
