<p><p>The Automatic Die-Cutter market comprises various types, including Rotary Die Cutter and Flat Bed Die Cutter. Rotary Die Cutters are characterized by the rotary motion of a cylindrical die, allowing efficient high-speed cutting of materials like paper or cardboard. On the other hand, Flat Bed Die Cutters use a flat plate with a stationary die to cut materials with precise accuracy. Both types are widely used in industries such as packaging and printing to streamline the cutting process and enhance productivity.</p></p>
