Full round tinned copper bars have a completely circular shape, providing uniformity and ease of installation. Slightly round edge tinned copper bars have a rounded shape, but the edges are not as pronounced as the full round bars. This makes them suitable for applications where a more streamlined appearance is desired. Square tinned copper bars have a right-angled shape and are typically used in applications where stability and durability are crucial.</p></p>
