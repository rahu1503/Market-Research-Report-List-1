<p><p>The industrial UAV market can be broadly categorized into two types: airplanes and multicopters. Airplanes are fixed-wing UAVs that resemble traditional aircraft and are capable of long-range flights, high speeds, and extended endurance. They are often used for large-scale aerial mapping, surveillance, and cargo transportation. Multicopters, on the other hand, are rotary-wing UAVs that utilize multiple rotors to hover, maneuver, and perform vertical take-offs and landings. They are typically used for close-range inspections, aerial photography, and agricultural applications due to their stability and maneuverability.</p></p>
