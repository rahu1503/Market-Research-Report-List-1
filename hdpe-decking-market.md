<p><p>HDPE Decking Market Types - Capped composite and Uncapped composite - refer to different types of materials used in the manufacturing of decking products. Capped composite decking has an additional protective layer that helps resist staining, fading, and mold growth, making it ideal for high-traffic areas and in regions with harsh weather conditions. On the other hand, uncapped composite decking lacks this protective layer, making it more cost-effective but also more susceptible to damage and discoloration over time.
