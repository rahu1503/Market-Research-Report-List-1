<p><p>The Personal Emergency Response System (PERS)/Medical Alert System market consists of three main types: Landline PERS, Mobile PERS, and Standalone PERS. Landline PERS operates through a landline telephone connection, allowing users to connect with emergency response teams by pressing a button on the pendant or wristband they wear. Mobile PERS enables individuals to access emergency services outside their homes using cellular technology. Standalone PERS functions independently without requiring a telephone line or cellular connectivity, often utilizing wireless communication. These systems are designed to provide immediate assistance in case of emergencies, ensuring the safety and well-being of users.</p></p>
