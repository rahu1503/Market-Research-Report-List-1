<p><p>The epoxy adhesive market can be categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component epoxy adhesives are ready to use and do not require any mixing or additional curing agent. They offer convenient and easy application, making them suitable for various applications. On the other hand, two-component epoxy adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that need to be mixed before use. This type offers a longer pot life, allowing for more complex bonding and curing processes. Two-component epoxy adhesives are commonly used in industrial and construction applications.</p></p>
