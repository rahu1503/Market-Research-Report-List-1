<p><p>The rail vehicle toilet system market includes different types of toilets, namely the vacuum toilet system and the pressurized water flushing toilet system. The vacuum toilet system uses suction to remove waste efficiently, reducing water consumption. On the other hand, the pressurized water flushing toilet system operates by forcefully flushing waste with water. Both systems aim to provide sanitary and convenient toilet facilities in rail vehicles, while the vacuum toilet system offers water-saving advantages, the pressurized water flushing toilet system ensures effective waste disposal.</p></p>
