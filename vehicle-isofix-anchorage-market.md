<p><p>The Vehicle ISOFIX Anchorage market comprises two main types: Forward facing CRS (Child Restraint System) and Backward facing CRS. Forward facing CRS refers to child seats that are designed to be secured in the vehicle facing the front. These seats are generally suitable for older children who have outgrown rear-facing seats. On the other hand, backward facing CRS are child seats that are installed in the vehicle facing the rear. These seats are specifically designed for infants and young children, providing better safety by supporting their head, neck, and spine in case of an impact.</p></p>
