<p><p>Redox Flow Batteries (RFBs) are energy storage systems that use redox reactions to charge and discharge electricity. One type of RFB is the Vanadium Redox Flow Battery, which uses vanadium ions in two separate tanks as the electrolyte. This design allows for high efficiency, long lifespan, and scalable capacity. Another type is the Hybrid Flow Battery, which combines the advantages of both RFBs and lithium-ion batteries. It uses a redox active species in the positive electrolyte and lithium ions in the negative electrolyte, providing enhanced performance and flexibility.</p></p>
