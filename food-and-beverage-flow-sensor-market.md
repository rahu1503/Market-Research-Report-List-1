<p><p>The Food and Beverage Flow Sensor market can be categorized into three main types: Mass Flow Sensor, Electromagnetic Flow Sensor, and Others. Mass Flow Sensor measures the mass of fluid passing through the sensor, providing accurate measurements. Electromagnetic Flow Sensor uses magnetic fields to determine the flow rate of conductive fluids. The Others category includes various types of flow sensors such as ultrasonic, thermal, and vortex flow sensors. These sensors play a crucial role in monitoring and controlling the flow of food and beverages in the production process.</p></p>
