<p><p>The Carbon Nanotube (CNT) Dispersions Market is categorized into three types based on the structure of the nanotubes: Single-walled Nanotubes (SWNTs), Double-walled Nanotubes, and Multi-walled Nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs consist of a single cylindrical layer and exhibit unique properties suitable for electronics, optics, and sensors applications. Double-walled Nanotubes comprise two concentric layers, offering improved electrical conductivity compared to SWNTs. MWNTs consist of multiple layers stacked concentrically, providing enhanced mechanical strength and thermal conductivity. These different types cater to diverse industry requirements, enabling advanced technological applications.</p></p>
