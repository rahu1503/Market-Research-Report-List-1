<p><p>PVC film for the medical market encompasses various types, including rigid PVC films, monomeric flexible PVC films, polymeric flexible PVC films, and others. Rigid PVC films offer high strength and durability, commonly used for packaging medical devices. Monomeric flexible PVC films are pliable and easy to handle, suitable for applications like blood bags and IV bags. Polymeric flexible PVC films provide enhanced flexibility and elongation, often used for wound dressing and surgical drapes. The "others" category includes specialized PVC films for specific medical applications, such as implantable devices or catheters, offering unique properties for specific purposes.</p></p>
