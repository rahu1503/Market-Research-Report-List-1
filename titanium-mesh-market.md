<p><p>The titanium mesh market consists of three main types: weaved mesh, stamped mesh, and expanded mesh. Weaved mesh is created by interlacing titanium wires together, providing a strong and durable mesh structure. Stamped mesh is produced by stamping patterns onto titanium sheets, resulting in a mesh with specific designs or shapes. Expanded mesh is made by cutting and stretching titanium sheets, enhancing its strength and flexibility. These different types cater to various applications and industries, offering a range of options for customers in the titanium mesh market.</p></p>
