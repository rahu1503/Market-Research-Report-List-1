<p><p>The gantry waterjet cutting machine market can be categorized into two types, namely pressurized water and abrasive mixture. In pressurized water cutting, a high-speed water jet is used to accurately cut various materials. On the other hand, abrasive mixture cutting involves the addition of an abrasive material, such as garnet, to the water jet. This abrasive mixture increases cutting efficiency, allowing for the precise and efficient cutting of harder materials like metal and stone. Both techniques offer unique advantages and cater to different applications within the cutting industry.</p></p>
