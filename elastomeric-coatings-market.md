<p><p>Elastomeric coatings are a type of protective coating that provides flexibility, durability, and weather resistance to various surfaces. The market for these coatings can be categorized into three types: solvent elastic coatings, emulsion elastic coatings, and inorganic polymer elastic coatings. Solvent elastic coatings are solvent-based and provide excellent adhesion to different substrates. Emulsion elastic coatings are water-based and offer good crack bridging capabilities. Inorganic polymer elastic coatings are based on inorganic polymers and provide exceptional durability and color retention, making them suitable for harsh environments.</p></p>
