<p><p>The Modified Silicone Adhesive market consists of two types: Neutral Cure and Moisture Cure. Neutral Cure adhesives use moisture in the air to cure and form a bond. They are typically used for sensitive applications, as they don't release any harmful byproducts during curing. On the other hand, Moisture Cure adhesives cure when exposed to atmospheric moisture. These adhesives are known for their fast curing time, making them suitable for various applications. Both types offer reliable bonding properties and find applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
