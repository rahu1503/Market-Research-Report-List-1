<p><strong>The Boat Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cranking or Starting Boat Batteries</li><li>Deep-Cycle Boat Batteries</li><li>Dual Purpose Boat Batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat batteries market comprises three main types of batteries. Cranking or starting boat batteries are designed to provide a powerful burst of energy to start the boat's engine. Deep-cycle boat batteries are designed to deliver a steady amount of power over a longer period, making them suitable for accessories like lights or trolling motors. Dual-purpose boat batteries combine elements of both cranking and deep-cycle batteries, offering both starting power and sustained energy for onboard electronics. These different types cater to diverse boat power needs and usage requirements.</p></p>
