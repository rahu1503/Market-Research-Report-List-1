<p><p>Cancer stem cells (CSCs) are a small population of cells within a tumor that possess unique self-renewal and differentiation capabilities. CSCs are believed to be resistant to conventional cancer therapies, leading to their identification as a target for novel treatment approaches.</p><p>The future outlook for the cancer stem cells market is promising, with significant growth expected in the coming years. Advancements in stem cell research and understanding of the mechanisms of CSCs have led to the development of targeted therapies specifically designed to eliminate these cells.
