Protein-based substitutes use ingredients like soy and whey protein to mimic the creamy and smooth properties of fats. Fat-based substitutes are made from vegetable oils and are used to replace solid fats like butter or margarine. These substitutes offer a healthier alternative to conventional fats while maintaining taste and texture in food products.</p></p>
