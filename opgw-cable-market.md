<p><p>OPGW cable is used in the power utility industry for transmitting high-voltage electricity. There are two types of OPGW cables: layer stranding structure and central tube design. Layer stranding structure cables have multiple layers of steel and aluminum wires wrapped around a central strength member. This design provides good mechanical protection and high electrical conductivity. Central tube design cables have a central tube that contains optical fibers for communication purposes. This design offers higher fiber count and better protection against moisture. Both types of cables play crucial roles in power transmission and communication.</p></p>
