<p><p>There are two main types of Maracuja oil in the market: cold-pressed and hot-pressed. Cold-pressed Maracuja oil is extracted from the seeds of the passion fruit using a slow process that involves no heat, preserving its natural properties. This type of oil is typically more expensive due to the careful extraction method. In contrast, hot-pressed Maracuja oil is obtained by subjecting the seeds to high temperatures during the extraction process. This method is quicker and cheaper, but it may result in a lower quality oil with reduced beneficial properties.</p></p>
