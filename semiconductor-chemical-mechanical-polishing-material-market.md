<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chemical Mechanical Polishing Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMP Slurry</li><li>CMP Pad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) material market consists of two major types: CMP slurry and CMP pad. CMP slurry is an abrasive fluid used in the CMP process to remove unwanted materials from semiconductor surfaces. It contains abrasive particles, chemicals, and surfactants to achieve the desired polishing effect. On the other hand, CMP pad is a soft polymeric material placed on the polishing machine to evenly distribute the slurry and apply pressure on the semiconductor surface during CMP. Both CMP slurry and CMP pad play crucial roles in achieving precise and efficient semiconductor polishing.</p></p>
