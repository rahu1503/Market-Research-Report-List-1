<p><strong>The Automotive Digital Key Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RKES</li><li>PKES</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Digital Key market comprises two main types: Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES) and Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES). RKES allows users to remotely lock and unlock their vehicles using a key fob or smartphone app. It uses radio frequency technology to communicate with the vehicle and provides convenience and security to users. On the other hand, PKES offers a seamless and hands-free experience. It automatically detects the key fob's proximity and unlocks the car when the user approaches it. This technology enhances convenience while ensuring the safety of the vehicle.</p></p>
