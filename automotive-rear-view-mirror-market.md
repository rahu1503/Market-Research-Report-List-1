<p><p>The automotive rear-view mirror market consists of four main types: flat mirror, convex mirror, aspheric mirror, and wide-angle mirror. Flat mirrors provide a regular reflection, while convex mirrors have a curved surface that increases the field of view but distorts the image. Aspheric mirrors have a complex shape that reduces blind spots and enhances safety. Wide-angle mirrors offer an extensive view, making it easier to detect vehicles in adjacent lanes. These different types cater to the diverse needs of drivers, improving visibility and ensuring safer driving experiences.</p></p>
