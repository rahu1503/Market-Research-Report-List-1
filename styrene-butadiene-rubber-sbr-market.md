There are different types of SBR in the market, including SBR, E-SBR, and S-SBR. SBR refers to the general class of SBRs, commonly used in tires, conveyor belts, and footwear. E-SBR, or Emulsion SBR, is made by polymerizing butadiene and styrene in the emulsion form, mostly used for tire treads and mechanical goods. S-SBR, or Solution SBR, is produced through a solution polymerization process and is primarily used in high-performance tires for improved fuel efficiency and grip.</p></p>
