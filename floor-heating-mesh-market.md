<p><p>The floor heating mesh market encompasses two main types: square mesh and rectangular mesh. Square mesh refers to a type of wire mesh that is uniform and symmetrical in shape, featuring equal spacing between wires horizontally and vertically. Rectangular mesh, on the other hand, consists of wires arranged in a rectangular pattern, with varying spacing between the horizontal and vertical wires. These mesh types are commonly used in floor heating systems to efficiently distribute heat and enhance the overall performance of the heating system.</p></p>
