<p><p>Cyclohexane Dimethanol (CHDM) is a chemical compound that is used in various industries, such as textiles, coatings, and plastics. The market for CHDM is categorized based on the cis/trans ratio, which refers to the arrangement of the two methanol groups on the cyclohexane ring. A cis/trans ratio of 35:65 means that 35% of the CHDM molecules have the methanol groups on the same side, while 65% have them on opposite sides. A ratio of 50:50 indicates an equal distribution of both arrangements. Other market types refer to CHDM with different cis/trans ratios that do not fall into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
