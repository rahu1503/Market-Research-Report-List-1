<p><p>Halogen-free data cables are cables that are manufactured without the use of halogen-based materials, specifically halogen-based flame retardants. Halogen-based materials, such as bromine and chlorine, are commonly used as flame retardants in various applications, including cables. However, when these halogens burn, they release toxic gases and create environmental hazards.</p><p>The demand for halogen-free data cables is increasing due to growing awareness about the harmful effects of halogens on the environment and human health. These cables offer several advantages, including reduced toxicity, better safety, and improved fire resistance. They are widely used in applications where fire safety is a primary concern, such as in buildings, transportation, and industrial sectors.</p><p>The future outlook of the halogen-free data cable market is expected to be positive.
