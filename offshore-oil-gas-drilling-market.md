<p><p>Offshore oil and gas drilling is conducted using different types of drilling platforms, including jackups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jackups are mobile platforms equipped with legs that are lowered to the sea floor, providing stability during drilling operations. Semi-submersibles are semi-submerged vessels that are either anchored or dynamically positioned, able to drill in deeper waters. Drillships are self-propelled vessels that are designed to operate in ultra-deep waters and are equipped with their own drilling equipment. These market types cater to different drilling requirements and water depths, offering flexibility and efficiency in offshore drilling operations.</p></p>
