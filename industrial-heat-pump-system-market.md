<p><p>The industrial heat pump system market consists of various types of heat pumps that help in generating heat or cold energy for industrial processes. Air to water cylinder heat pumps extract heat from ambient air and transfer it to water for heating purposes. Groundwater heat pumps extract heat from underground water sources for heating and cooling. Split air-to-water heat pumps have separate indoor and outdoor units for efficient heat transfer. Exhaust air heat pumps recover waste heat from exhaust air and use it for heating purposes.</p></p>
