<p><p>Synthetic polythiols, also known as mercaptans, are a type of organic compounds that contain a sulfhydryl (-SH) group. One of the key factors driving the market is the increasing demand for adhesives and sealants in various industries. Synthetic polythiols are widely used as curing agents in adhesives, providing excellent bonding properties. The growing construction industry, particularly in emerging economies, is fueling the demand for adhesives, thus driving the market growth.</p><p>Another factor contributing to the growth of the synthetic polythiols market is the increasing focus on sustainability and environmental regulations. Synthetic polythiols are considered eco-friendly as they do not contain harmful substances such as lead, chromate, or mercury. The stringent regulations imposed by various governments to reduce the carbon footprint are boosting the demand for sustainable materials, including synthetic polythiols.</p><p>Furthermore, the expanding automotive industry is also expected to drive the market growth. Synthetic polythiols find applications in rubber manufacturing for tire production, where they act as chain transfer agents. With the increasing demand for automobiles worldwide, the demand for synthetic polythiols is projected to grow substantially.</p><p>In conclusion, the global synthetic polythiols market is anticipated to experience significant growth in the coming years.
