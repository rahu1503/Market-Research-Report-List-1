<p><p>The Vehicle Speed Sensor (VSS) market includes three types of sensors: optical, hall-type, and magnetic-electric. Optical VSS utilizes light to measure the speed of a vehicle, while Hall-type VSS employs the Hall effect to detect changes in magnetic fields. Magnetic-electric VSS combines both magnetic and electric properties to determine vehicle speed. These sensors play a crucial role in providing accurate speed information to various vehicle systems, enabling features like cruise control and ABS. The market for VSS is diverse, with different types of sensors catering to different vehicle requirements and technologies.</p></p>
