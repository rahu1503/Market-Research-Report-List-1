<p><p>The Aluminium Oxide Ceramics market can be categorized into two types: Coarse Varieties and Fine-grained Varieties. Coarse Varieties are characterized by larger grain sizes, resulting in higher porosity and lower mechanical strength. These ceramics are typically used in applications that require good thermal shock resistance and insulation properties. On the other hand, Fine-grained Varieties have smaller grain sizes, providing superior mechanical strength and hardness. They are ideal for applications that demand high wear resistance, such as cutting tools and abrasion-resistant components.</p></p>
