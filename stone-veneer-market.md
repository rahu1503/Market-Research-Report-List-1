Stone veneer offers the look of natural stone at a lower cost, making it an attractive choice for architects, builders, and homeowners.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing trend towards sustainable construction practices is expected to boost the market. Stone veneer is considered a more sustainable option compared to natural stone, as it eliminates the need for quarrying and reduces waste.
