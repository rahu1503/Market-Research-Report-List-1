<p><p>FIBC bulk liners are specialized bags designed to transport and store various types of products. EVOH liners are known for their excellent barrier properties, preventing the permeation of oxygen and moisture. Foil liners are made of aluminum foil, providing additional protection against light, moisture, and gases. Baffled liners have internal baffles that stabilize the load and prevent shifting during transportation. Antistatic liners are designed to dissipate static electricity, ensuring safety during the handling of sensitive products. Conductive liners are similar to antistatic liners but offer higher conductivity. Other market types include liners designed for specific industry needs, such as food-grade liners or liners with barrier properties against specific chemicals.</p></p>
