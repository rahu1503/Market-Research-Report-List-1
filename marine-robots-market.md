<p><strong>The Marine Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autonomous Underwater Vehicles</li><li>Unmanned Surface Vehicle</li><li>Underwater Gliders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Robots market consists of three main types: Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs), Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs), and Underwater Gliders. AUVs are self-propelled robots that are used for underwater exploration, data collection, and inspections. They operate without human intervention, navigating through the water using sensors and mapping technologies. USVs, on the other hand, are vehicles that operate on the water's surface without a crew onboard. They are often used for surveillance, monitoring, and research purposes. Lastly, Underwater Gliders are buoyancy-driven robots that move through the water by changing their buoyancy and pitch. They are commonly used for data collection, oceanography research, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
