<p><p>Thin film solar modules are a type of solar technology that uses thin layers of semiconductor materials to convert sunlight into electricity. There are three main types: CdTe thin-film, CIS/CIGS thin-film, and a-Si thin-film. CdTe thin-film modules are made using cadmium telluride as the semiconductor material. CIS/CIGS thin-film modules use copper indium selenide or copper indium gallium selenide. a-Si thin-film modules use amorphous silicon. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of efficiency, cost, and manufacturing process.</p></p>
