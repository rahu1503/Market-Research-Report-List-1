<p><p>The Industrial Handwheels market consists of various types, including spoked handwheels, solid handwheels, and arm handwheels. On the other hand, solid handwheels have a single, continuous structure, offering durability and strength. Arm handwheels are designed with an extended arm or lever, allowing for increased leverage and easier operation.
