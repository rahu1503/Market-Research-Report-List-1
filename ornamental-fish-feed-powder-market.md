<p><p>The ornamental fish feed powder market can be categorized into two types: plant-based and microbial-based. Plant-based feed powder is derived from various plant sources, such as soybean meal or wheat germ, and is known for its nutritional content and ease of digestibility. On the other hand, microbial-based feed powder is produced through microbial fermentation processes. It offers high protein content and is often supplemented with essential amino acids and fatty acids. Both types cater to the unique dietary requirements of ornamental fish, providing ample nutrients for their growth and overall well-being.</p></p>
