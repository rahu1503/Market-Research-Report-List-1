<p><p>Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) sponge brushes are available in two types: roll shape and sheet shape. Roll shape brushes are cylindrical in form, resembling a traditional paint roller. They are designed for easy gripping and rolling on surfaces, making them suitable for larger areas and quick application. Conversely, sheet shape brushes are flat and rectangular, resembling a typical household sponge. They are more versatile and can be easily maneuvered into corners and tight spaces, offering more precise and controlled application. These different market types cater to diverse usage preferences and offer convenience in various painting tasks.</p></p>
