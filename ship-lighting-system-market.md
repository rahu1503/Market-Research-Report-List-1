<p><p>The ship lighting system market offers three main types of lights: halogen lights, xenon lights, and LED lights. Halogen lights use a tungsten filament enclosed in a halogen gas-filled bulb, providing high intensity and a warm color tone. Xenon lights use xenon gas to produce a bright and white light, suitable for long-range visibility. LED lights, on the other hand, use light-emitting diodes to emit light, offering energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and customizable colors. Each type caters to different ship lighting requirements and preferences in terms of brightness, color, and efficiency.</p></p>
