<p><p>The industrial chillers market consists of three main types: air chillers, water chillers, and evaporative condensed chillers. Air chillers use atmospheric air to cool down industrial processes or equipment. Water chillers, on the other hand, use water as a coolant to remove heat from processes. Lastly, evaporative condensed chillers utilize the process of evaporation to cool down hot water. These three types of chillers cater to various industrial needs and play a crucial role in maintaining optimal temperature conditions in industrial settings.</p></p>
