<p><strong>The Automotive Catalytic Converter Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-way Catalysts (TWC)</li><li>Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC)</li><li>Lean NOx Trap (LNT)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Catalytic Converter Equipment market comprises various types of catalysts used to reduce harmful emissions from vehicles. Three-way Catalysts (TWC) are used in gasoline-powered vehicles to convert carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxides, and hydrocarbons into less harmful substances. Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC) are specifically designed for diesel engines to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions. Lean NOx Trap (LNT) catalysts are used in combination with LNC to store and convert nitrogen oxides. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) catalysts are utilized in diesel engines to convert nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water. Lastly, Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) helps in oxidizing carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons from diesel engine exhausts.</p></p>
