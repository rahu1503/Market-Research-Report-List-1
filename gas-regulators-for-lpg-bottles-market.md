<p><p>Gas regulators for LPG bottles come in three market types: low pressure, middle pressure, and high pressure regulators. </p><p>Low pressure regulators are designed for household or small-scale usage, supplying gas at a pressure usually below 2.8 kPa. Middle pressure regulators are used for medium-scale applications such as camping stoves or heaters, providing gas at a pressure ranging from 2.8 to 35 kPa. </p><p>High pressure regulators cater to large-scale industrial usage and can handle pressures exceeding 35 kPa. These regulators ensure safe and efficient gas flow for various purposes within each designated pressure range.</p></p>
