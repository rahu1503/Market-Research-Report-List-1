<p><p>Metalloid gaskets are used in various industries to create a tight seal between two surfaces. There are different types available in the market. Spiral-wound gaskets consist of a metal strip wound in a spiral shape with a filler material. Camprofile (grooved) gaskets have grooves on their sealing faces. Tanged graphite sheet gaskets are made of graphite sheets with a metal reinforcement. There are also other types of metalloid gaskets that serve different purposes. These gaskets ensure leakage prevention, withstand high temperatures and pressures, and are commonly used in the oil and gas, chemical, and automotive industries.</p></p>
