<p><p>Fork Truck Market Types refer to two specific categories of forklift trucks that are widely used in different industries. The first type is the Counterbalanced Forklift Truck, which is designed with a weight at the back to counterbalance the weight of the load being lifted. This allows for greater stability and prevents tipping. The second type is the Warehouse Forklift Truck, which is specifically designed for use in confined spaces within warehouses or storage facilities. It is built with a compact and maneuverable design to efficiently navigate narrow aisles and tight spaces while handling loads.</p></p>
