<p><strong>The Agriculture Carbamide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granular</li><li>Prilled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agriculture carbamide market comprises two types: granular and prilled. Granular carbamide is a solid form of urea fertilizer that is characterized by its hard, free-flowing particles. It is often used for surface or top dressing applications. Prilled carbamide, on the other hand, consists of small spherical particles that are more uniform in size and shape. This type is commonly used for blending with other fertilizers or for deep placement in the soil. Both granular and prilled carbamide are widely utilized in the agricultural industry to enhance crop growth and productivity.</p></p>
