<p><p>Electrofusion systems are used in the joining of plastic pipes and fittings. AC waveform and DC waveform are the two main types of electrofusion systems available in the market. AC waveform systems use an alternating current to produce heat, allowing the fusion of plastic materials. Both types have their advantages and applications, and their choice depends on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
