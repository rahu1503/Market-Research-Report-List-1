<p><p>Copper Based Powder market encompasses two main types - Copper Powder and Copper Alloy Powder. Copper Powder refers to a fine, brownish-orange powder composed of pure copper, primarily used in electrical conductors, printing inks, and chemical compounds. On the other hand, Copper Alloy Powder consists of copper blended with other elements like zinc, tin, nickel, or aluminum, resulting in enhanced properties such as increased strength and corrosion resistance. Copper Alloy Powder finds applications in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, electronics, and construction, due to its improved characteristics.</p></p>
