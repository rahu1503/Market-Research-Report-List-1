<p><p>The Commercial Baking Equipment market can be categorized into various types including Commercial Convection Ovens, Deck Ovens, Roll In Rack Ovens, Revolving Ovens, and other miscellaneous equipment. Commercial Convection Ovens are designed to circulate hot air inside, ensuring even baking. Deck Ovens have multiple baking shelves stacked on top of each other, allowing for simultaneous baking at different temperatures. Roll In Rack Ovens are large-scale ovens that can accommodate multiple racks simultaneously. Revolving Ovens have rotating carousels or shelves for uniform baking. The "Other" category encompasses various baking equipment not falling into the aforementioned types.</p></p>
