<p><p>Exhaust sensors are vital components in the automotive market that help monitor and control exhaust emissions. The Exhaust Temperature & Pressure Sensor measures the temperature and pressure levels in the exhaust system, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency. The O2 Sensor monitors the oxygen levels in the exhaust gases, enabling the engine control unit to adjust the fuel-air mixture for improved fuel efficiency. The NOX Sensor detects nitrogen oxide levels in the exhaust, helping to regulate emissions. The MAP/MAF Sensor measures the intake manifold pressure or airflow, aiding in precise engine control. Other exhaust sensors include the particulate matter sensor and the exhaust gas recirculation sensor.</p></p>
