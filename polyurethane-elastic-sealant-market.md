<p><p>Polyurethane elastic sealants are widely used in various industries for their strong bonding and flexibility. One-component polyurethane elastic sealants are pre-mixed and ready to use, simplifying the application process. They can be applied using a cartridge or gun, and cure upon exposure to moisture in the air. Two-component polyurethane elastic sealants require mixing of two separate components before use. This type offers a faster curing time and higher strength bond, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types provide excellent adhesion, durability, and resistance to weather, chemicals, and UV radiation.</p></p>
