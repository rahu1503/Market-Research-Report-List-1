<p><p>Air springs are used in vehicles to provide a cushioning effect and enhance ride comfort. There are various types of air springs available in the market. Convoluted air springs, also known as bellows, are made of multiple layers of rubber and are commonly used in heavy-duty trucks and buses. Sleeve air springs are constructed with a single flexible rubber sleeve and find applications in passenger cars and light trucks. Other types of air springs include rolling lobe air springs and double convolution air springs, which are suitable for different vehicle types and suspension systems.</p></p>
