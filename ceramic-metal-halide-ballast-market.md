<p><p>The Ceramic Metal Halide Ballast market is divided into two types: Double Ended and Single Ended. Double Ended refers to a type of ballast where both ends of the ceramic metal halide lamp have an electrical connection. This allows for better heat dissipation and higher efficiency in lighting. On the other hand, Single Ended ballasts only have one connection point, limiting their heat dissipation and efficiency. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications, depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
