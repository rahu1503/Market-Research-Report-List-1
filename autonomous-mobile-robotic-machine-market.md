<p><strong>The Autonomous Mobile Robotic Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LiDAR Based</li><li>LiDAR+Vision Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Autonomous Mobile Robotic Machine market is divided into two types: LiDAR Based and LiDAR+Vision Based. LiDAR Based machines rely solely on LiDAR technology for navigation and perception. LiDAR, or Light Detection and Ranging, uses laser beams to measure distances and create a detailed 3D map of the surroundings. On the other hand, LiDAR+Vision Based machines combine LiDAR technology with computer vision algorithms. This combination allows machines to not only perceive their environment through LiDAR but also analyze visual data from cameras, enabling more accurate perception and decision-making capabilities.</p></p>
