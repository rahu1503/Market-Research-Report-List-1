<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The EAA adhesive market consists of three main types: film grade, extrusion coating grade, and dispersible grade. Film grade EAA adhesives are used in the production of flexible packaging films, providing excellent adhesion properties. Extrusion coating grade EAA adhesives are primarily used in the coating of various substrates, offering high bond strength and heat resistance. Dispersible grade EAA adhesives are designed to disperse easily in water, making them suitable for applications in paper industries, textiles, and hygiene products. Each grade targets specific industries and offers unique characteristics to fulfill their adhesive requirements.</p></p>
