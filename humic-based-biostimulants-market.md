Humic acid is a complex organic compound that enhances soil health, nutrient availability, and plant growth. Fulvic acid is a subset of humic acid that is smaller in size and easily absorbed by plants, aiding in nutrient uptake and plant metabolism. Potassium humate is a potassium salt of humic acid, promoting root development, stress tolerance, and overall plant vigor. These biostimulants are used in agriculture to improve crop productivity and sustainability.</p></p>
