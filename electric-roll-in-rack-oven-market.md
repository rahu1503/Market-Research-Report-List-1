<p><p>Electric roll-in rack ovens are commonly used in commercial bakery settings to bake large quantities of goods. There are two types of electric roll-in rack ovens on the market: static and rotating. Static roll-in rack ovens have stationary racks, while rotating roll-in rack ovens have racks that rotate during baking. Static ovens are suitable for baking delicate and even items, while rotating ovens are preferred for products that require more even browning or multiple racks with shorter baking times. These ovens provide efficiency and convenience for high volume baking needs.</p></p>
