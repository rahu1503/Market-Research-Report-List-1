<p><p>Form handling machines refer to devices used for processing paper documents in various industries. There are different types of form handling machines, including bursters, decollators, and others. Bursters are machines that separate continuous forms into individual documents by tearing the perforated edges. Decollators, on the other hand, remove carbon-backed or carbonless sheets from continuous forms, creating separate sets of documents. The "Others" category includes additional machines like collators, document finishers, and imprinters, which handle various tasks such as sorting, stacking, binding, and imprinting information on forms. These machines enhance document processing efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
