Lipase, on the other hand, is an enzyme that degrades various types of lipid-based plastics. Cutinase is an enzyme that helps break down the waxy layer called cutin found in plants, which can also be found in some plastic films. These enzymes play a crucial role in breaking down different types of plastics, contributing to the research and development of more sustainable waste management and recycling methods.</p></p>
