<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Systems market consists of two main types: the Pneumatic EGR Valve and the Electric EGR Valve. A Pneumatic EGR Valve operates using air pressure to control the recirculation of exhaust gases, reducing nitrous oxide emissions and improving fuel efficiency. On the other hand, an Electric EGR Valve utilizes an electric motor to control the valve's opening and closing, providing more accurate control of the recirculation process. Both systems aim to reduce harmful emissions and enhance overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
