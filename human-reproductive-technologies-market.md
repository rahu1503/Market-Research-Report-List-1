<p><p>Human Reproductive Technologies refer to various methods used to assist individuals and couples in achieving pregnancy. In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) involves fertilizing eggs and sperm outside the body and transferring resulting embryos into the uterus. Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) involves directly injecting a single sperm into an egg to facilitate fertilization. Zygote Intrafallopian Transfer (ZIFT) transfers fertilized embryos into the fallopian tubes. Gamete Intrafallopian Transfer (GIFT) transfers unfertilized eggs and sperm into the fallopian tubes. Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) involves placing sperm into the uterus during ovulation. Contraceptive Technology market includes various methods such as hormonal contraception and barrier devices that prevent pregnancy.</p></p>
