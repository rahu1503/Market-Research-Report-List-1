<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li><li>Type Ⅲ</li><li>Type IV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airframe De-icing Products market encompasses four main types: Type I, Type II, Type Ⅲ, and Type IV. Type I de-icing fluids are typically made of propylene glycol and are used to remove already accumulated frozen contaminants from an aircraft's surface. Type II fluids provide both de-icing and anti-icing capabilities, preventing ice buildup and enhancing performance. Type Ⅲ de-icing fluids are formulated with high-viscosity additives, ensuring a longer-lasting protective barrier against ice formation. Lastly, Type IV de-icing fluids are polymer-based and offer extended anti-icing properties while being environmentally friendly.</p></p>
