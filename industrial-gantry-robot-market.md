<p><p>The Industrial Gantry Robot market can be broadly classified into two types: Open Gantry Robot and Closed Gantry Robot. Open Gantry Robots are designed with a framework that leaves the top part open, allowing easy access and visibility to the working area. They are suitable for applications that require frequent material handling. On the other hand, Closed Gantry Robots are enclosed structures where all sides are covered. This design provides added safety, protection, and precision, making them perfect for applications that involve delicate or hazardous operations.</p></p>
