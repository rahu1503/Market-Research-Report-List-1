<p><p>Astringents are substances used to contract body tissues and control bleeding. Different market types of astringents include thrombin-based, oxidized regenerated cellulose based, combination, gelatin-based, and collagen-based astringents. Thrombin-based astringents utilize the enzyme thrombin to promote clot formation. Oxidized regenerated cellulose based astringents promote hemostasis by forming a gel-like barrier. Combination astringents combine multiple ingredients to enhance their efficacy. Gelatin-based astringents use gelatin to form a physical barrier on wounds. Collagen-based astringents utilize collagen as a hemostatic agent to promote clotting and control bleeding.</p></p>
