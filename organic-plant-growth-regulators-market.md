<p><p>Organic plant growth regulators are substances that aid in the growth and development of plants without the use of harmful chemicals. There are three main types of organic plant growth regulators: auxins, cytokinins, and gibberellins. Auxins promote cell elongation and stem/root growth, cytokinins stimulate cell division and promote plant tissue differentiation, while gibberellins promote stem elongation and seed germination. These organic plant growth regulators are widely used in the agriculture industry to enhance crop yield, improve plant vigor, and regulate various physiological processes in plants.</p></p>
