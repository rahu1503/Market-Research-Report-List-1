<p><p>The Silver Conductive Paste market can be categorized into two main types: Front Side Silver Paste and Back Side Silver Paste. Front Side Silver Paste is typically used in the production of solar cells, specifically for the front side electrode formation. It helps in enhancing the electrical conductivity and adhesion of metal contacts. On the other hand, Back Side Silver Paste is used for backside electrode formation, allowing for improved conductivity and adhesion on the rear side of solar cells. Both types of pastes contribute to the overall efficiency and performance of solar cells.</p></p>
