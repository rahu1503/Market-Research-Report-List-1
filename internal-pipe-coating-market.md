<p><p>Internal pipe coating is a protective layer applied to the interior of pipes to shield them from corrosion, scaling, leakage, and other damage. The coating can be made of various materials, such as epoxy, polyurethane, and others, and is typically applied using spray or brush techniques. It acts as a barrier between the pipe surface and the liquid or gas flowing through it, preventing corrosion and other issues that can compromise the integrity of the pipes.</p><p>The global internal pipe coating market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
