Veneering refers to the application of a thin layer of lithium disilicate on the surface of a dental restoration. Crown refers to a single-tooth restoration made entirely of lithium disilicate material, while bridge denotes a restoration involving multiple teeth. These different market types cater to various dental needs, providing aesthetic and functional solutions for diverse dental restorations.</p></p>
